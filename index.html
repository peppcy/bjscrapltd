What We Do

Residential / Commercial Services

In Port Harcourt and communities in Rivers Sate, our organization provides quick and effective garbage collection and disposal services to residential flats, hotels, restaurants, banks, lock-up stores, and workplaces.

Waste Recovery & Recycling

B&J Scrap Limited is presently studying and investing in resource recovery strategies to collect recyclable materials from the Port-Harcourt City Waste Transfer Station site, such as plastic, paper and cardboard, steel, aluminum, drink cans, and food waste. The long-term goal of our recycling activities is to lead the world in the three R's: Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle.

Waste Transfer and Landfill Facility Management

B&J Scrap Limited specializes in the day-to-day administration and maintenance of Garbage Transfer Loading Stations (TLS) and Landfills, the foundation of a successful solid waste management system that takes waste that cannot be recycled or processed in other disposal methods. Our company's highest operational standards and efficiency in this area have been demonstrated by the introduction of a new Waste Transfer Station bulk haulage technology known as Waste Ejector Trailers, which can haul approximately 30 metric tonnes of waste from the transfer station and eject the waste at a speed of approximately 3 minutes.

We have effectively integrated human resources with current technologies.

As a result, we are now the preeminent and top garbage management firm in Nigeria.

Industrial Services

In Nigeria, we provide garbage collection and disposal services to companies, building and demolition sites, retail malls, and marketplaces.

For the collection and disposal of construction/demolition debris and other specialty garbage, we employ specialized contemporary waste collecting equipment such as Roll-On-Roll-Off and Skip-Bin. We also provide industrial cleaning of vessels and tanks, poluution control & decontamination.

Equipment leasing & Logistics

B&J Scrap limited also imports and supplies heavy equipment and vehicles to Nigeria. We have provided compactors, roll-on-roll-off and skip bin trucks, garbage transfer stations, ejector bulk transport trailers, waste bins and containers, and waste recycling equipment.

Oil-Spill Clean-up & Remediation

B&J Scrap Limited offers industry knowledge and experience in hydrocarbon polluted site rehabilitation. We clear marsh and land areas with hydrocarbon pollution at depths more than one meter. We also offer coastline cleanup and thorough mangrove vegetation removal. Our professional spill response crew is well trained and prepared to address oil pollution accidents on land, in marshes, and offshore.
